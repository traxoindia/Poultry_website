Poultry Care Guide

The Science of Vaccine Stress Management

Strategic supplement protocols before and after vaccination dramatically reduce bird stress and improve immune response.

💡 Pro Tip for Farmers

When vaccinating birds, always administer StressNill Pro 24 hours before and after each vaccination session for optimal antibody formation.

1

Pre-Vaccination — 24 hrs Before

Administer StressNill Pro in drinking water to prime electrolyte balance and reduce anticipatory stress.

2

Vaccination Day

Ensure birds are well-hydrated. Handle calmly and minimize environmental disruptions during dosing.

3

Post-Vaccination — 24–48 hrs After

Continue StressNill Pro support to sustain antibody response and accelerate immune recovery.

4

Liver Support Protocol

Follow up with Wings Livoton for 5–10 days to restore hepatic function and promote optimal growth.
